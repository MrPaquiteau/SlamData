Project Presentation: Tennis Grand Slam Results Web Platform

The goal of this project is to develop a website centered around an engaging topic. We have chosen to focus on the results of various tennis tournaments.

Additionally, our objective is to integrate the data into a PhpMyAdmin database, enabling us to perform various tasks:

  • A data exploration section, utilizing OLAP operators, for example (featured on our website in the "Achievements" section).
  • A data integration section, where tasks such as adding a match or a player can be carried out.

Our Data Source

We have sourced all of our data from a single platform: "Kaggle." We have also verified the accuracy of the data to ensure its reliability before integration.
